With an architectural attitude rooted in the pursuit of excellence, we operate as a design studio that connects space, furniture, and installation through rigorous architectural experimentation.

Space remains in place, yet it continuously evolves through its users. We engage with these organic transformations and design environments that generate a broader, positive impact on society.

Through the exploration of new perspectives and materiality, we strive to create spaces that resonate deeply with human perception and everyday life.

Studio Zak
is a Seoul-based
design studio.

We work at the intersection of space, furniture, and installation — connecting disciplines through rigorous architectural experimentation.

Founded with a commitment to excellence, we engage with the organic transformations of space and its users. Our practice is driven by the belief that well-designed environments generate a broader, positive impact on the lives of those who inhabit them.

Each project begins with deep observation — of site, material, light, and the people who will dwell within the space.
